It's a robot! It's a Java Class! No, it's Elizabeth Larson, coding extraordinaire!
Experienced in computer animation, website design, and cyber security, Elizabeth has it all. Her super speed allows her to finish scientific endeavors as fast as a bolt of lightning. When she enrolled in her high school's computer science course, she knew she wanted to pursue a technological career. Throughout high school, she's conducted experiments about computer animation, ocular health, improving online shopping, and cyber security. She's programmed robots, created mazes, designed websites, managed databases, and much more! HTML, PHP, Java, CSS, and Basic codes fill her computer, ready for future tweaking and reference. Elizabeth has also used her technological power to give back to the community. She has been teaching a community computer class for two years and hosts an annual Hour of Code night at her school. Elizabeth is currently working on an app to promote a community festival and conducting a research project centered around password security. In the future, she hopes to attend a four-year university and further her studies in computer science.
